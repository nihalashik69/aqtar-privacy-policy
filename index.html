Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AQTAR ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (such as your name or email).

However, we may collect anonymous data through tools like Firebase for the purpose of usage statistics and crash reports.

2. Location Data

We may use your device’s location to display your current position on the map and to show nearby points of interest. Your location is not stored or shared with third parties.

3. Firebase & Analytics

We use Firebase, a service by Google, for anonymous analytics and performance monitoring. This may include non-personal data like app usage, crash logs, and technical device information.

No personally identifiable information is collected or stored through Firebase services.

4. No Account or Login Required

The app does not require you to create an account or log in. We do not collect your email, phone number, or other personal identifiers.

5. No Ads

We do not show any third-party advertisements in this app.

6. Data Retention

We do not store any user data on our own servers. All analytics data collected via Firebase is subject to Google’s data retention policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

9. Compliance with Google Play Policies

This app complies with Google Play’s Data safety section requirements. Users are informed about what data is collected, how it’s used, and how it’s secured.

10. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit. No sensitive user data is stored locally or remotely by us.

11. Consent

By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.
